My name is Andrei Turcas, I am a 20 years old piano enthusiast playing in my spare time whenever I get the chance because it is my most valuable hobby besides my computer science studies. I have been practicing this instrument since 7 years old, but I only started to get more serious about it recently and I am looking forward to improve as much as possible.

Life is like a piano. The white keys represent happiness and the black shows sadness. But as you go through life's journey, remember that the black keys also makes music.
